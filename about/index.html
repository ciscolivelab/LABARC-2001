    About The Lab

    Learning Objectives

    Upon completion of this lab, you will be able to : - Use localized policy to deploy OSPF policy - Use localized policy to deploy QoS policy - Use centralized policy to deploy a Hub & Spoke Topology - Use centralized policy to deploy Application Aware Routing

    Catalyst SD-WAN Manager

    Cisco Catalyst SD-WAN Manager provides centralized configuration, management, operation, and monitoring across the entire SD-WAN fabric. You will comeplte all lab tasks using Catalyst SD-WAN Manager.

    Localized Policy Oveview

    Localized policies are deployed and enforced locally on SD-WAN Edge devices. There are two type of localized policies. - Localized Control Policy - Localized Data Policy In this lab, you wil deploy OSPF localized control policy and QoS localized data policy using Catalyst SD-WAN Manager.

    Centralized Policy Overview

    Centralized policy is policy applied to the whole SD-WAN fabric. Centralized policies are deployed on SD-WAN Controllers. Depends on the type of policy, enforcement of centralized policy can be either on SD-WAN controller or on SD-WAN Edge devices. There are two type of centralized policies. - Centralized Control Policy - Centralized Data Policy

    In this lab, you will deploy Hub & Spoke centralized control policy and AAR centralized Data Policy using Catalyst SD-WAN Manager.
